Research

"If we knew what it was we were doing, it would not be called research, would it?"
-Albert Einstein

A simple idea to connect students using common catchphrases that summarizes our attitude towards school led to the creation of our brainchild, Teekle. Teekle aimed to create common topics among friends (or even strangers) through catchy slogans and create a brand that is uniquely from our alma mater, Nanyang Technlogical University.

Being an entrepreneur is never easy, but it is a wonderful opportunity to prove yourself. I have been fortunate to be a part of the amazing Tee!kle team as a Founder and Head of Sales & Marketing division of the venture.
